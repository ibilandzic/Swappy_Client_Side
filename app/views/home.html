What is Swappy?

Swappy is website which helps you to exchange or give away your old clothes. It's is very easy to use, simply register, browse ads and post your own. If there is an item you like, contact the owner and arange shipping.

How much do I have to pay?

Swappy is free of any charge. We don't take any fee nor we handle shipping. Shipping is handled between person who posted the ads and the one who wants the item.

How to register?

Registration is very simple. Fil in the registration form and log in. Only personal information that is displayed is your username and your email for contact.

How to make ad?

Go to "Your account" --> "Your ad" and click on "New ad" button. This will open form for creating new ad.

Can I mark item as favorite?

Of course you can! Each item ad has heart shaped icon. By clicking that icon you saved that ad as your favorite. If you change your mind simply remove that item from your list.

Can I search items?

Yes! Items are listed by categories. If you want, you can search in each category by item type.
